## SHADOW DATA EXPANSION — 3 NEW LAYERS (2026-05-04 final — commit 0a7c4d3)

Full audit of shadow data coverage found 3 critical blind spots blocking
profit-lock and exit calibration. All three shipped in one commit.

### Gap 1 — Zero visibility on what happens AFTER we exit (HIGHEST VALUE)

**New stage: `post-exit-watch`**

After every close (profit-lock, stop, settle) on live-prediction / structural /
live-swing / pre-game strategies, the bot now watches the price for 20 minutes
via an `exitPriceWatch` Map and logs a shadow record when the window closes.

Fields logged:
- `exitPrice` / `entryPrice` / `exitReason`
- `maxPriceAfterExit` — highest price seen in 20min after exit
- `minPriceAfterExit` — lowest price seen in 20min after exit
- `upleftOnTable` — how much more was available if we'd held
- `priceGoesHigherAfter` — did price keep rising after we sold?
- `priceReversesBelowExit` — did price drop below our exit price?
- `exitWasNearPeak` — was our exit within 3¢ of the post-exit peak?

**Why:** Without this, we cannot answer "was our profit-lock threshold right?"
even after weeks of MFE data. MFE captures the peak DURING the trade. This
captures what happened AFTER we left. Directly feeds per-strategy R:R tuning.

### Gap 2 — Claude hold decisions at profit-lock never recorded

**New stage: `profit-lock-hold`**

When Claude evaluates a profit-lock and says HOLD, now logs a shadow record
with: `weAtHold`, `profitCents`, `holdEV`, `gameStage`, `claudeReasoning`,
`period`, `scoreDiff`. `ourPickWon` fills in at game settle.

**Why:** We have no data on whether Claude's hold decisions are correct.
After ~50 samples, can measure "hold at WE≥70% wins X%" and tune the
profit-lock prompt per strategy. Currently completely uncalibratable.

### Gap 3 — Sportsbook-gap detector discarded all below-threshold gaps silently

**New stage: `sportsbook-gap-candidate`**

`detectPregameSportsbookGap` now logs every gap ≥ 2pt (above AND below the
5pt threshold) with: `gapPt`, `sportsbookProb`, `kalshiPrice`,
`clearsThreshold`, `rejectReason` (gap-below-5pt / price-below-30c / etc).

**Why:** Post parser fix (6cb3219), detector is live for the first time. We
have zero data on the actual gap distribution it's seeing. If it's seeing
3-4pt gaps on 5 games/day, that's potentially missed alpha. The threshold
is a guess — now it's measurable.

### LIVE-SWING KEY FINDINGS

#### Entry price is everything

| Bucket | n | WR | Hit +12¢ | Avg MFE | Hold-to-settle EV |
|--------|---|----|----------|---------|------------------|
| ≤60¢ | 19 | 78% | **47%** | +13.6¢ | +$0.138/contract |

| 71–80¢ | 42 | 69% | **0%** | +4.1¢ | **-$0.053/contract ← LOSING** |
| 81¢+ | 3 | 100% | 0% | +2.7¢ | small n |

**71–80¢ bucket is structurally negative EV.** Break-even WR at price p = p itself.
At 75¢ you need 75% WR; we have 69%. The +12¢ profit-lock NEVER fires (0/42). These
are hold-to-settle bets that shouldn't be taken unless WE gap is ≥6pt above price.

#### Period breakdown (MLB only)

| P2 | 38 | 76% | 5% | +5.8¢ |

P1 entries are at low prices (50–60¢) with game wide open — true swing territory.
P2/P5 entries are at high prices (65–80¢) where market barely moves mid-game.

#### Time of day (critical)

| Window | n | WR | Avg entry |
|--------|---|----|-----------|
| Afternoon (<5pm ET) | 54 | **64%** | 69¢ |

Same avg entry price, very different WR. Afternoon MLB is noisier — 1-run lead in P5
at 2pm ET is more volatile than 1-run lead in P5 at 9pm ET.

May 3 concrete example: 7 consecutive P5 diff=1 afternoon shadow losses, 2:35–2:48 PM ET,
all same game cluster where the trailing team came back. Classic afternoon noise.

#### Best combo: ≤65¢ + evening + MLB
n=18, **WR=88%**, hit +12¢=33%. The sweet spot.

#### Score diff: 1-run leads hit +12¢ MORE than 2-run
- diff=1: 71% WR, hit+12¢=20% (13/66)
- diff=2: 71% WR, hit+12¢=5% (2/38)
Same game WR but 1-run leads entered cheaper → more room to swing.

#### Line-mover signal: NOT helpful for swing
